Our Mission & Vision

We are committed to creating a world where every animal is treated with compassion and respect

Creating a Better World for Animals

Founded in 2010, Paws & Hearts Animal Charity has been at the forefront of animal rescue and rehabilitation. Our mission is to rescue animals from abuse, neglect, and homelessness, providing them with medical care, rehabilitation, and loving forever homes.

We believe that every animal deserves a chance at a happy, healthy life, and we work tirelessly to make that a reality through our comprehensive programs and community outreach.

  • Rescue animals from abusive situations
  • Provide medical treatment and rehabilitation
  • Find loving forever homes for our animals
  • Educate the community about animal welfare
  • Advocate for stronger animal protection laws
